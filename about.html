About Signal Flag Outdoors

Adventure should feel clear, welcoming, and possible.

Based in Coeur d'Alene, Idaho, Signal Flag Outdoors helps beginners step into outdoor movement with practical instruction and a steady, encouraging approach.

Built for people at the starting line.

The name Signal Flag points to communication, awareness, and readiness. Those ideas shape every lesson and outdoor experience: know the plan, understand the risk, move with intention, and look out for each other.

  • Professional but approachable instruction
  • Beginner-centered pacing
  • Local outdoor education for North Idaho
  • Experiences that combine movement, skill, and community

Core offerings

  • Beginner longboard lessons
  • Outdoor skills sessions
  • Community rides
  • Adventure-based group experiences
  • Custom outings for families and small groups

Beginner does not mean unprepared.

Signal Flag Outdoors keeps the tone friendly while treating safety, route choice, communication, and progression with care.
