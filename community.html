Ride together

Community rides for new and returning riders.

Signal Flag Outdoors creates positive group experiences where people can meet, learn local routes, and build outdoor confidence at a comfortable pace.

What to expect

Community rides are beginner-aware, route-conscious, and built around clear expectations before wheels roll.

01

Route Briefing

Each ride starts with pace, distance, terrain, regroup points, and safety expectations.

02

Beginner Pace

The goal is connection and confidence, not speed. Riders are encouraged to stay within their comfort zone.

03

Outdoor Skills

Rides can include short moments on etiquette, weather awareness, gear, and route planning.

Community values

  • Respectful riding and shared trail awareness
  • Encouragement for first-timers
  • No pressure to perform beyond your ability
  • Leave-no-trace outdoor habits
  • Local connection in North Idaho
