What a beginner lesson includes

  • Pre-ride safety check and comfort conversation
  • Simple skill progression from flat ground to gentle movement
  • Board control, stopping, turning, and awareness drills
  • Local route guidance for continued practice
  • Optional next-step plan after the lesson

Good fit for

  • Families looking for an active experience
