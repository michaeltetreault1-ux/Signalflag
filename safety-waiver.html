Safety first, always

Clear expectations before every lesson or ride.

Outdoor activities and longboarding involve risk. Signal Flag Outdoors reduces risk through preparation, appropriate terrain, safety gear, honest communication, and beginner-aware instruction.

Required gear

  • Helmet for all longboard sessions
  • Closed-toe shoes with secure fit
  • Water and weather-appropriate clothing
  • Protective pads strongly recommended for new riders
  • Board check before riding

Participation expectations

  • Stay within your current ability level
  • Follow route, regroup, and stop instructions
  • Tell the instructor about injuries or concerns
  • Respect shared paths, pedestrians, and local rules
  • Pause when conditions are unsafe

Waiver note

Before participating, riders may be asked to complete a liability waiver and provide emergency contact information. For participants under 18, a parent or guardian should complete required forms.

This page is a website placeholder for general safety expectations and is not a substitute for a final legal waiver. Have any final waiver language reviewed by a qualified local professional before using it for paid activities.

Before you arrive

  • Share your experience level honestly
  • Bring required gear
  • Check weather and dress for conditions
  • Ask questions before the session starts
